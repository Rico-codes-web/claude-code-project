MMITS Hiraya Transit — Data Privacy Notice

In compliance with Republic Act No. 10173, the Data Privacy Act of 2012, and its Implementing Rules and Regulations, MMITS Hiraya Transit collects and processes the following personal data:

• Name & Age — for personalized experience and leaderboard.
• Usual destination — for commute suggestions and traffic insights.
• GPS location — to display your position on the map and assist emergency services when SOS is activated.
• Incident reports — submitted voluntarily, shared with MMDA for verification.

Your data is used solely for app functionality and NCR traffic safety improvements. It is not sold to third parties. You may request deletion at any time via Profile → Privacy & Data.

By proceeding, you consent to data collection as described above. This app is a prototype; data is stored locally in your browser session only.
